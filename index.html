Drawing inspiration from the game Journey By Thatgamecompany and Nintendo's The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom in both visual style (stylised 3d) and gameplay mechanics, Distant Wind is as much a game that heavily emphasises movement and environment traversal as it is a piece of artistic media.
Not only does this freedom allow for a unique approach to storytelling, but also heavily backs the artistic aim of portraying the place that people have the universe.

The 'intention' of Distant Wind is to look at human emotions, experiences and relationships from an objective perspective, putting a whimsical spin on the absurdity of much of our lives. A core concept of Distant Wind's design is procedural generation, allowing players to progress the game in quite literally any direction they want.
